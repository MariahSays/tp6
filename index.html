15 Minute Healthy Cashew Chicken

  • Prep time: 5 minutes
  • Active time: 10 minutes
  • Total time: 15 minutes

Now that you know what you're in for - let's get started with this delicous, fast, and healthy cashew chicken. Get ready to turn up the heat!

Ingredients

Sauce

  • 1 tablespoon dark soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1/3 cup chicken stock
  • 1 tablespoon Chinese black vinegar
  • 1.5 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • Pinch white pepper

Cashew Chicken

  • 1 cup Raw cashews
  • 1 lb boneless skinless chicken thighs cut into 1-inch cubes - Salt & Pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 bunch green onion - 1” segments
  • 4 cloves garlic, rough chop
  • 1-inch knob ginger, grated
  • 1 rib celery, medium dice
  • 1 green bell pepper, ½” dice
  • 1/2 cup water chestnuts, halved or quartered

Kitchen Equipment

  • whisk or fork
  • 9" x 9" baking pan
  • Wok
  • Measuring cups
  • Measuring spoons
  • Spatula
  • Knife
  • Hand Grater
  • small bowl

Directions

Sauce

  1. In a small bowl, whisk in all the ingredients until combined.

Cashew Chicken

  1. Preheat oven to 350F. Place cashews on a 9" x 9" baking sheet and bake for 5 - 8 minutes, or you can toast them in a pan.
  2. Season your chicken thighs cubes with salt; In a wok, heat oil, add chicken, and let it sear for 6 to 8 minutes, often turning, so it is evenly brown and cooked all the way through. Remove chicken from wok and set aside for later use
  3. In your wok, add oil; once hot, add onions, stir fry for about one minute or until you get a light charred. Next, add garlic, ginger and stir fry for 30 seconds.
  4. Then, add your celery, bell pepper and stir fry aggressively for 30 seconds or until your peppers start to soften. Next, add your chicken back and chestnuts, stir fry for 30 seconds to one minute and add your sauce; bring it to a boil, stir fry for 25 more seconds or until thickened and glossy.
